Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pixell Global ("Pixell", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use sayou, including the sayou website at sayou.dev, the sayou hosted service, APIs, and integrations (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Workspace content: Files, documents, metadata, and key-value data you store in your sayou workspaces.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting us for support or feedback.

1.2 Information from Third-Party Integrations

When you connect third-party services via OAuth, we may receive:

Service Data accessed Purpose
Google Profile information (name, email), authorized Google Workspace data (e.g., Google Docs, Google Sheets content you explicitly grant access to) Import and sync content you choose to store in your sayou workspace
Notion Profile information (name, email, workspace name), authorized pages and databases you select during the connection flow Import and sync content you choose to store in your sayou workspace

We only access the specific data and permissions you authorize during the OAuth consent flow. We do not access data beyond the granted scopes.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: How you interact with the Service, including API calls, features used, and workspace activity metadata (not content).
  • Device and connection information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and referring URLs when you visit our website.
  • Logs: Server logs for security, debugging, and service reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Service — storing your workspace content, syncing data from integrations, and serving API requests
  • Maintain and improve the Service — fixing bugs, optimizing performance, and developing new features
  • Communicate with you — responding to support requests, sending service-related announcements, and (with your consent) product updates
  • Ensure security — detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations — fulfilling our legal and regulatory requirements

3. Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

sayou's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only use Google user data for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.
  • We do not use Google user data to serve advertising.
  • We do not sell Google user data to third parties.
  • We do not use Google user data for purposes unrelated to providing or improving the Service.
  • Human access to Google user data is limited to what is necessary for security purposes, to comply with applicable law, or when aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.

4. Notion Integration — Data Handling

When you connect your Notion workspace:

  • We only access pages and databases you explicitly authorize during the OAuth flow.
  • Data imported from Notion is stored in your sayou workspace and treated as your content.
  • We do not share your Notion data with third parties.
  • You can disconnect your Notion integration at any time, which stops future data access. Previously imported content in your sayou workspace can be deleted by you at any time.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: We use third-party infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting and database services) to operate the Service. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually bound to protect it.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • Safety and security: We may share information to protect the safety of our users, enforce our Terms, or protect our legal rights.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With your consent: We may share information when you explicitly direct us to do so.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Where We Store Data

Hosted Service data is stored on servers in the United States (AWS us-east-2 region). If you self-host sayou using the open-source software, your data is stored wherever you choose to deploy it.

6.2 How We Protect Data

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS for all API communications)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Access controls and authentication for all Service endpoints
  • Regular security monitoring and incident response procedures
  • Organization-level data isolation — your data is architecturally separated from other organizations' data at the storage and query level

6.3 Data Retention

We retain your workspace content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Version history is retained as a core feature of the Service. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information and account.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format. sayou's file-based architecture means your workspace content is already in human-readable, portable formats (Markdown with YAML frontmatter).
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke any consent you have given for data processing.
  • Disconnect integrations: Revoke OAuth access for any connected third-party service at any time through your account settings or through the third-party service directly.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@pixell.global.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The sayou website may use essential cookies for functionality (such as authentication sessions). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how the website is used.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Open-Source Software

If you use the self-hosted open-source version of sayou, your data remains entirely on your own infrastructure. This Privacy Policy applies only to the hosted Service and website operated by Pixell Global.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:
