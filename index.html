FAQ

Questions

What can sayou actually do?

Connect your tools, then ask sayou agent anything. It searches across your Notion pages and Slack conversations to give you answers grounded in your own work.

Is my data safe?

sayou is open source. Every line of code is public. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, never used for training, and you can delete everything at any time.

What tools does it connect to?

Notion and Slack today. Google Drive, Gmail, Linear, and GitHub are coming next. For developers, sayou also works with Claude Code, Cursor, and other AI coding tools.

Is the free plan actually free?

Yes. One connected source, unlimited files, no credit card. Upgrade when you need more sources or real-time sync.

Can I self-host?

Yes. The core is Apache 2.0. Run it on your own infrastructure. The hosted version just means you don't have to.

What if I cancel?

Your data is removed from sayou. Your original files in Notion and Slack are never touched.
